WE ARE
PECPL

PECPL is prominent globally for the Detailed Project Report (DPR) level investigations in the mining & metals, hydro & thermal energy, infrastructure, transmission & distribution line and other core sector industries. Our predominant area of services include soil Investigation, consultancy, topographical & contour survey for a green/brown field projects, geophysical survey, hydro-geological investigations, mineral exploration & resource evaluation as per UNFC Classification, Geotechnical investigations for Mine/Plant/Energy/Infrastructure Projects & for any other project proponent. We provide world class services for Detail Technical & financial evaluation of the Projects of any magnitude. Ours is an innovation company, updated with all modern technique for sub-surface investigation and committed to fulfill the client’s satisfaction & expectations. Thus, we provide turnkey DPR level investigation services for all Green field and Brown field projects & to any other project at construction level. The clientele range included PSU’s, Large Private Sectors, MNC’s, Government Departments, National & International Universities etc.
