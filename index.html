Company Profile

PECPL is prominent globally for the Detailed Project Report (DPR) level investigations in the mining & metals, hydro & thermal energy, infrastructure, transmission & distribution line and other core sector industries. Our predominant area of services include soil Investigation, consultancy, topographical & contour survey for a green/brown field projects, geophysical survey, hydro-geological investigations, mineral exploration & resource evaluation as per UNFC Classification, Geotechnical investigations for Mine/Plant/Energy/Infrastructure Projects & for any other project proponent.
